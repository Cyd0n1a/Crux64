Int. Scene start. Game starts. Lower third:

MOUNT XERXES: BASE CAMP

Scene 01: The Morning After

[SCENE START]

VISUAL:
The screen is dark. The UI slowly fades in, minimalistic and unobtrusive.
A faint, warm orange glow pulses softly in the center of the screen.

AUDIO:
The rhythmic, comforting crackle of a dying campfire.
The high-pitched, gentle hiss of a camping kettle just starting to boil.
A gentle alpine breeze rustling nylon tent fabric.

VISUAL: The player's eyes open (fade from black). First-person perspective. We are looking out through the half-open flaps of a well-worn mustard yellow canvas tent. Frost clings to the edges of the fabric. Outside, the sky is a bruised, beautiful twilight purple, just beginning to bleed into dawn gold.

Looming in the immediate distance, dominating the entire upper half of the screen, is MT. XERXES. It is massive, jagged, and serene.

UI Notes: Dialogue box appears, (background: like a burst of static on an old television). As the text appears, letter by letter, typewriter style, a sound effect is heard.

UI PROMPT:
[ Push Forward to exit tent ]

The player crawls out of the tent.

AUDIO:
The crunch of boots on frost-hardened earth. The wind is slightly louder now, carrying the distant sound of shifting ice.

The player stands before the campfire.

UI PROMPT:
[ Press (Interact) to pour tea ]

The player picks up the kettle and pours steaming water into an enamel mug. As the steam rises, a gentle phone jingle cuts through the quiet.

AUDIO:
The static crackle of a Bluetooth earpiece connecting.

MAYA (V.O.)
(Through the headset. Her voice is warm, tired, and laced with gentle static)
Hey. Tracking beacon says you're awake. Or at least, your backpack is.

PLAYER
(Voice is slightly raspy from sleep)
I'm awake, Maya. Just pouring some tea.

MAYA (V.O.)
Good. It's freezing out there. Did you sleep at all?

PLAYER
A few hours. The fire kept me company.

(The player looks up at the peak of Mt. Xerxes. The morning sun just hits the highest ridge, painting it a brilliant, fiery orange.)

PLAYER
It's bigger than it looked in the brochures.

MAYA (V.O.)
(Soft chuckle)
Yeah, well, Xerxes doesn't mess around. Neither do you, apparently. I still can't believe you're actually out there. You know... you didn't have to do this alone. I could have taken the time off work. We could have done the lower trails.

PLAYER
I know. Thank you. But... I need it to be just me right now. Me, the rock, and you in my ear.

MAYA (V.O.)
(Sighs, affectionately but sadly)
You've been doing the solo act since you were nine years old. You know you're allowed to let people carry the weight with you sometimes, right?

PLAYER
(Takes a slow sip of tea. The audio highlights the comforting sound of the sip, followed by a long exhale)
I'm okay, Maya. Really. The silence out here... it's loud, but it's good.

MAYA (V.O.)
Okay. I won't push. How's the tea?

PLAYER
Terrible. I brought his favourite blend. That awful Earl Grey with the dried lavender.

MAYA (V.O.)
(Her voice catches slightly, dropping to a softer register)
Oh. He would have loved that you brought it. Though he probably would have forced you to drink the whole pot while lecturing you about the tannins.

PLAYER
(A sad, fond smile in their voice)
Yeah. He would have.
(Pause)
I'm going to take it up there, Maya. To the summit. Like we planned before... before the diagnosis.

MAYA (V.O.)
Take your time with it. The mountain isn't going anywhere. There are four major rest points on your route. Check in with me when you hit the first ridge, okay?

PLAYER
Will do.

MAYA (V.O.)
Be safe. Talk soon.

AUDIO:
Beep. The headset disconnects, plunging the player back into the vast, beautiful acoustics of the mountain valley. The wind whispers through the pines.

UI PROMPT:
[ Press (Interact) to pack up Base Camp ] [ Press (C UP) to approach the wall ]

[SCENE END]
